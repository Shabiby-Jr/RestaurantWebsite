BON APPETIT RESTAURANT

Welcome to Bon Appetit Restaurant, your NO. 1 source for all delicious meals. We are dedicated to giving you the very best tastes of life, with a focus on quality, customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 2019 by Omar Khalid, Bon Appetit Restaurant has risen to the top in no time! The founder and his team got the impetus to turn hardwork and inspiration into a booming restaurant. We now serve all of our customers with utmost love and style. One of the very nicest things about life is that we must regularly stop whatever we are doing and devote our attention to eating. That's why we are here! For you! We are located on Jomo Kenyatta Avenue, opposite KCB Bank, Mombasa. We hope you enjoy our meals and services as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, don't hesitate to contact us.
